Getting started

From equation to insight
in four steps.

No setup, no install. Just open and learn.

1

Open any demo

Click a demo card above. It opens in your browser instantly — no server, no build, no internet needed.

2

Switch to Equation-driven

Use the Data Source toggle at the top of the sidebar. Built-in data is the default; Equation-driven is one click away.

3

Type your equation

Enter any math expression — sin(x), x^2 - 4, exp(-x^2)*cos(4*x). Use ^ for powers, * for multiply, pi and e as constants.

4

Press Play

Watch the model train. The gold dashed curve is your equation; the purple curve is the model's attempt to mimic it.

2D Boundary
y - sin(5*x) > 0
For KNN/SVM/Decision Tree. Sine-wave class boundary.
Ring
x^2 + y^2 - 0.3 > 0
Donut-shaped class region. Linear SVM will struggle.
Checkerboard
sin(x*5)*cos(y*5) > 0
Complex 2D pattern. Decision Tree handles it well.
Diamond
abs(x-0.5)+abs(y-0.5)-0.3 > 0
L1-norm boundary. CART's axis-aligned splits fit naturally.
